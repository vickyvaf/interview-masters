# 🍏 Script, Motion & Voiceover Guide: Apple & Jitter Style "Interview Masters"

**Durasi Video:** 25 Detik (5 Scene x 5 Detik / 750 Frame @ 30fps)  
**Style & Aesthetic:** Jitter Motion Design / Apple Aesthetic (Minimalist Pitch Black `#000000`, Stepped Motion, No-Gradient High Contrast, Sleek Glassmorphism)  
**Audio Track:** Percussive Tech Beat (128 BPM) + Fast Snaps & Sub-bass Drops (`apps/remotion/public/backsound.mp3`)  
**Voiceover Tone:** Short, Punchy, Confident, & Irresistible (Singkat, To-the-point, dan Menggiurkan)

## 🎬 Breakdown Detail Scene per Scene

### Scene 1: The Role Scroller (00:00 - 00:05 / Frame 0 - 150)
* **Visual & Motion:**
  * Background: Pitch Black (`#000000`).
  * Scroller 25 role pekerjaan meluncur bertahap dari bawah ke atas dalam kontainer miring `-7.5°` (*Jitter Stepped Motion*: 500ms move + 250ms hold).
  * **Auto-Select di Titik Tengah:** Saat role melintas di garis tengah layar, efek blur otomatis menjadi `0px` (*crisp white* `#FFFFFF`) & opasitas `100%`. Role di atas/bawahnya diberi blur bertingkat (`10px`, `20px`, `30px`...).
  * Dimulai dari detik ke-0 pada role ke-3 (`"DevOps Engineer"`).
* **Text on Screen:** Single scrolling role list (`Frontend Engineer`, `DevOps Engineer`, `Product Manager`...)
* **VO Bahasa Indonesia:** *"Apapun role impianmu..."*
* **VO English Version:** *"Whatever your dream role is..."*
* **SFX:** Mechanical slot reel tick & crisp snap sound.
