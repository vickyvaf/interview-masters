# Stitch Prompt — Interview Masters Landing Page

## Prompt

Design a landing page for **Interview Masters**, an AI-powered mock interview practice platform. The design should be clean, professional, and modern. No gradients. Solid colors only. Medium-sized elements throughout — nothing too small or too large.

---

### Brand & Style

- **Color palette**: Dark navy background (`#0F172A`), indigo primary (`#6366F1`), amber accent (`#F59E0B`), off-white text (`#F8FAFC`), emerald for success states (`#22C55E`)
- **Typography**: Sans-serif, medium weight. Headings bold but not oversized. Body text comfortable to read.
- **Aesthetic**: Dark mode. Flat design. Solid colors, no gradients. Clean spacing with clear visual hierarchy.
- **Border radius**: Slightly rounded corners (12px) on cards and buttons.

---

### Page Sections (top to bottom)

#### 1. Navigation Bar
- Logo text "Interview Masters" on the left
- Nav links in the center: Features, Pricing, About
- Two buttons on the right: "Log In" (outline) and "Start Free" (filled indigo)

#### 2. Hero Section
- Medium-sized headline: **"Practice Interviews. Get Real Feedback. Land the Job."**
- One-line subtext: "AI-powered mock interviews for any role — with instant, structured feedback."
- Two CTA buttons side by side: "Start Practicing Free" (indigo, filled) and "See How It Works" (outline)
- Below buttons: small social proof text — "Trusted by 5,000+ candidates across 20+ industries"

#### 3. Problem Section
- Section label: "WHY IT MATTERS"
- Three horizontal pain point cards, solid dark card background:
  - 🎯 **Unstructured prep** — Watching videos doesn't build real confidence
  - 💬 **No honest feedback** — Friends can't tell you what's really wrong
  - ⏱️ **No time to practice** — Coaches are expensive and hard to schedule

#### 4. How It Works Section
- Section label: "HOW IT WORKS"
- Three steps in a row with numbered icons:
  1. **Pick your role** — Select from 20+ job categories or paste a job description
  2. **Do the mock interview** — Answer AI-generated questions by text or voice
  3. **Get instant feedback** — See your score, structure gaps, and what to improve

#### 5. Features Section
- Section label: "WHAT YOU GET"
- 2x2 grid of feature cards (solid dark cards, indigo icon accent):
  - Role-specific questions
  - STAR method analysis
  - Progress tracking dashboard
  - Repeatable practice sessions

#### 6. Pricing Section
- Section label: "PRICING"
- Three pricing cards side by side — Free, Pro, Team:
  - **Free**: Rp 0/month — 3 sessions/month, basic feedback
  - **Pro**: Rp 99.000/month — Unlimited sessions, advanced feedback, analytics (highlight this card with indigo border)
  - **Team**: Custom — Bulk licenses, HR dashboard

#### 7. Testimonials Section
- Section label: "WHAT CANDIDATES SAY"
- Three testimonial cards in a row, solid dark background:
  - Short quote + name + role (e.g., "Fresh Grad → Product Manager at Tokopedia")

#### 8. Final CTA Section
- Bold headline: **"Your next interview is closer than you think."**
- Subtext: "Start with 3 free sessions. No credit card required."
- Single centered button: "Get Started Free" (indigo, filled, medium size)

#### 9. Footer
- Logo left, nav links center, copyright right
- Simple one-row layout

---

### Design Rules (Strict)

- ❌ No gradients anywhere — solid colors only
- ❌ No oversized hero text or tiny detail text — keep everything medium scale
- ✅ Dark mode as default
- ✅ Clean, generous whitespace between sections
- ✅ Consistent 12px border radius on all cards and buttons
- ✅ Indigo for primary actions, amber only for highlights/badges, emerald for success/positive signals
